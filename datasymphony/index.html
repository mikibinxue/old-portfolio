About

A Kinect data sonication project tracking locations, movement, and transit methods throughout New York City in a playful P5 visualization


The Sonic exploration is a product of self-tracking and self-exploration.
Through visualization and sound generation, the project is a review of my every day life, and serves as a incentive to explore more of New York City.
