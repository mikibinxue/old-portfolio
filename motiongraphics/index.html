Based on research on the 1980s aesthetic.

This motion graphics project is meant to represent the design style of the Memphis Design.
Memphis Design movement dominated the '80s with their crazy patterns and vibrant colors.
Many designers and architects from all around the world contributed to the movement in order to escape from the strict rules of modernism.
