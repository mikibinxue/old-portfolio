About

Ken Perlin developed the noise function while working on the original Tron movie in the early 1980s;
he used it to create procedural textures for computer-generated effects.
Perlin noise can be used to generate various effects with natural qualities, such as clouds, landscapes, and patterned textures like marble.

Fascinated by the beauty of the Noise() function in computer programming,
I decided to contextualize the function through physical fabrication.

The patterns are generated in openFrameworks with Noise() function.
